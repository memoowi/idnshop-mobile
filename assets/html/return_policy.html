Return Policy

At IDNShop, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return and refund policy.

Return Window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to return any eligible item(s). To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at email@email.com within the return window.

Eligible Items for Return

  • Unworn, unwashed, and undamaged items with original tags attached.
  • Items that are incorrect or damaged upon arrival (please contact us within 7 days of receiving the order to report such issues).
  • Items that are faulty or defective.

Ineligible Items for Return

  • Items that have been worn, washed, altered, or damaged due to user fault.
  • Items without original tags attached.
  • Items purchased during clearance sales (clearly marked as non-returnable).

Damage Caused by User Fault

  • Rips, tears, or stains caused by improper wear or washing.
  • Damage caused by misuse or neglect.
  • Normal wear and tear.

Return Process

  • Contact Customer Service: Contact our customer service team at email@email.com within the return window to initiate a return request.
  • Return Authorization: You will receive a return authorization email with instructions on how to return your item(s).
  • Safe Packaging: Pack the item(s) securely in the original packaging if possible.
  • Return Shipment: Ship the return package to the address provided in the return authorization email. You are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item is incorrect, damaged, or faulty.

Refunds

Once we receive your returned item(s) and verify that they meet the return criteria, we will issue a full refund to your original payment method within 5 business days. Please note that it may take additional time for your bank to process the refund.

Original Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item is incorrect, damaged, or faulty.

Exchanges

Unfortunately, we are not currently able to offer exchanges for returned items. If you would like a different size or color, you will need to place a new order.
